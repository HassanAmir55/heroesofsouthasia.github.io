WHAT WE DO

Elucidating this initiative's core principles and intended actions.

Our Principles

To shift the existing narrative about South Asians in Canada, we follow three guiding principles — Represent, Advocate, and Collaborate. Through our instagram page, we aim to educate Canadians of the voices and journeys of South Asian youth across the country. This includes celebrating their identities, challenges, and contributions. Social media has fueled and contributed to anti-south asian racism, we want to use those same channels to educate Canadians instead.

REPRESENT

We elevate authentic stories of South Asian youth that reflect our diversity — across regions, languages, religions, and experiences. Representation is the first step toward visibility and belonging.

ADVOCATE

We confront stereotypes and challenge systems of exclusion by sharing real-life struggles and successes. Our platform is a space for resistance and policy awareness.

COLLABORATE

We work with youth, educators, artists, and community leaders to create inclusive content and campaigns that amplify South Asian excellence and demand equity.
